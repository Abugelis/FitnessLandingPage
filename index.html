FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

DO I NEED PRIOR TRAINING EXPERIENCE?

No. The program is designed to meet you where you are. Whether you’re a beginner or returning after time off, progressions are built in so you can start safely and build confidence over time.

HOW MUCH TIME DO I NEED EACH WEEK?

Most workouts take 30–45 minutes. The focus is on efficient, structured training that fits into real life — not spending hours in the gym every day.

WHAT EQUIPMENT DO I NEED?

Very little. The program prioritizes bodyweight movements and simple equipment. If you have access to basic weights, great — but it’s not required to see results.

IS THIS PROGRAM ABOUT WEIGHT LOSS OR MUSCLE GAIN?

This program focuses on strength, movement quality, and consistency. Many people experience fat loss and muscle gain as a result, but the primary goal is building a strong, capable body.

CAN I ADAPT THIS AROUND MY SCHEDULE OR OTHER ACTIVITIES?

Yes. The structure is flexible and designed to complement your lifestyle. You can adjust training days, intensity, or volume without breaking the system.
